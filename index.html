💡 What is modeldock?

Running artificial intelligence on your own computer often feels difficult. You need to find files, move them to folders, and make sure your computer talks to the software correctly. modeldock removes these hurdles. It acts as a digital library for your AI models. You see what you have, download what you need, and start your projects without writing code.

🚀 Getting Started

Follow these steps to set up modeldock on your Windows computer. You do not need experience to get this running.

1. Check System Requirements

Your computer needs a few things to run AI models well. Ensure you have at least 8GB of RAM. We recommend a solid-state drive with 20GB of free space. If you have an NVIDIA graphics card, modeldock uses it to make your AI run much faster. Ensure your graphics drivers are up to date.

2. Download the App

Visit the official page to grab the latest installer. This file contains everything you need to begin.

Click here to visit the download page

3. Installation

Find the file you downloaded in your Downloads folder. Double-click the file to start the installation. Windows might show a security window. Click "More info" and then "Run anyway" if prompted. Follow the buttons on the screen to finish the setup. The app creates a folder on your computer to store your AI models.

📂 Finding and Using Models

Once you open modeldock, you see a search bar. Type the name of a model you want to use. The app shows a list of options. Click the "Download" button next to the model name. modeldock saves the file to your drive. Once the download ends, the "Load" button becomes active. Click it, and your model is ready.

🛠️ Managing Your Library

Models take up a lot of space. Use the "Library" tab to check which models you have installed. You can delete files to save space with one click. Use the "Check for updates" feature to keep your AI smart and current.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is this software safe? Yes. modeldock operates entirely on your computer. Your files stay on your hard drive and do not travel to the internet unless you choose to download a model.

Can I use different AI engines? Yes. modeldock connects to different technologies. You can switch between them in the settings menu.

How do I add new features? modeldock is an open project. If you have an idea, you can reach out on our GitHub page to suggest changes.
